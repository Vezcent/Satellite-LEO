1. TOM TAT
================================================================================

  S-MAS la mot ban sao so (digital twin) hoat dong ve tinh do trung thuc
  cao, su dung hoc tang cuong da tac tu (Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning
  - MARL) de tu dong toi uu hoa thoi gian song quy dao cua ve tinh vi mo
  quy dao thap (LEO). He thong mo phong tau vu tru PROBA-1 cua ESA
  (NORAD 26957) o do cao ~600 km, tich hop du lieu thoi tiet vu tru lich
  su thuc (2001-2020), ban do nguy co buc xa, va mo hinh luc can khi quyen
  da duoc xac thuc.

  Nen tang bao gom bon ngon ngu lap trinh xuyen suot pipeline sau giai doan:
  C++ (dong co vat ly), Python (huan luyen MARL), C# (bo dieu khien van hanh),
  va TypeScript/React (bang dieu khien WebGPU 3D). Ba tac tu AI khong dong
  nhat (Dieu huong, Quan ly Bus, Nhiem vu) phoi hop duoi bo giam sat an toan
  FDIR dua tren luat su dung thuat toan MAPPO voi Huan luyen Tap trung va
  Thuc thi Phi tap trung (CTDE).

  Dong co vat ly da duoc xac thuc voi 30 ngay du lieu TLE thuc cua PROBA-1
  su dung lan truyen SGP4, dat MSE 336,5 km^2 sau hieu chinh.

4. GIAI DOAN 1: DONG CO VAT LY C++
================================================================================

  4.1 Co hoc quy dao

    - Bo tich phan Runge-Kutta bac 4 (RK4) tai dt = 5,0 s
    - Trong luc Trai Dat: khoi diem + nhieu loan J2
    - Luc can khi dong: F_D = -0,5 * rho * Cd * A * v^2 * v_hat
    - Hieu chinh dong quay khi quyen: v_rel = v - (omega x r)
    - Luc day: vector 3D lien tuc voi buom ga [0, 1]

  4.2 Mo hinh khi quyen NRLMSISE-00 (Don gian hoa)

    - Nhiet do ngoai tang: T_inf = f(F10.7, F10.7_avg, Ap)
    - Profil nhiet do Bates-Walker
    - Mat do can bang khuech tan voi dieu che ngay/dem va vi do
    - He so nhan mat do = 0,01 (hieu chinh cho thoi gian song dai han)
      Xac thuc ngan han (30 ngay): 0,1 cho MSE=336,5 km^2
      Thuc te dai han (nhieu nam): 0,01 cho suy giam thuc te ~20+ nam tai 600km

  4.3 Cac he thong con duoc mo phong

    - SatelliteBus: pin mat troi GaAs (~90 W), pin Li-ion voi suy giam
      Arrhenius, chu ky nap/xa eclipse
    - Tang ngau nhien: nhieu cam bien, tre bo chap hanh (1-3 buoc),
      sai so bo chap hanh (+/-5%), troi Cd (buoc ngau nhien)
    - Bo tao SEU: su kien don xac suat khi qua SAA
    - Tam nhin tram mat dat: LoS hinh cau voi mat na nang 5 do

  4.4 API C (Xuat DLL)

    StatePacket: 184 byte, packed (phien ban 1)
    ActionPacket: 19 byte, packed (phien ban 1)

6. GIAI DOAN 3: TANG NHIEM VU
================================================================================

  6.1 Tac tu Nhiem vu (Dieu khien CHRIS)

    - Bat/tat tai trong quang hoc dua tren vi tri quy dao
    - Phat +50,0 diem cho chup anh muc tieu hop le
    - Phat -500,0 diem cho bat tai trong trong vung SAA

  6.2 Phoi hop Meta

    - Neu tac tu Bus bat deep_sleep, tai trong buoc tat
    - Bo giam sat FDIR ghi de tat ca tac tu khi DEGRADED/SAFE

  6.3 May trang thai FDIR

    Trang thai | Nguong SoC | Quyen AI
    -----------|------------|----------------------------------
    NOMINAL    | > 20%      | Tu chu hoan toan
    DEGRADED   | < 20%      | Payload OFF, luc day gioi han 30%
    SAFE       | < 10%      | Ghi de toan bo, buoc ngu sau
    RECOVERY   | > 15%      | Payload OFF, dang chuyen tiep lai

7. GIAI DOAN 4: BO DIEU KHIEN C#
================================================================================

  - Ung dung console .NET 10.0
  - Rang buoc P/Invoke toi smas_engine.dll voi [StructLayout(Pack=1)]
  - Suy luan ONNX Runtime cho 3 mo hinh tac tu
  - May chu WebSocket nhi phan bat dong bo tai ws://localhost:8765
  - Giao thuc: [Version(1B)|Seq(4B)|PayloadLen(4B)|Payload(98B)|CRC32(4B)]
  - Bo dem vong (32 khung) de quan ly ap luc nguoc
  - Mo phong suy giam mang (tre, jitter, mat goi)
  - Ghi log CSV va dong co phat lai ngoai tuyen
  - Che do tua nhanh (--skip N): mo phong tat ca buoc vat ly, chi phat
    moi N buoc. Vi du: --skip 1000 = 1000x toc do cho chay nhieu nam.
    Tham khao so buoc: 1 ngay = 17.280 | 1 nam = 6.307.200

8. GIAI DOAN 5: BANG DIEU KHIEN WEBGPU
================================================================================

  - Cong nghe: Vite + React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS v4
  - Render chinh: WebGPU voi shader WGSL
  - Du phong: WebGL2 voi shader GLSL (phat hien tu dong)
  - Giai ma do do (hook useTelemetry): phan tich DataView khong cap phat
  - Tu dong ket noi lai WebSocket: thu lai moi 2 giay neu controller
    chua chay (co the khoi dong dashboard truoc controller)
  - Thiet ke HUD: chu de glassmorphism khong gian sau, hieu ung xung FDIR
  - Bo dem Thoi gian Song: hien thi thoi gian da troi dang Xn Xd Xh
  - Chi bao VE TINH DA CHET voi nguyen nhan (tai nhap/pin/lien lac)
  - SoC, do cao, hanh dong tac tu, trang thai moi truong thoi gian thuc
  - Chuyen doi toa do: Lat/Lon/Alt -> ECEF (X, Y, Z)
