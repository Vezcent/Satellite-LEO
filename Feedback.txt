# S-MAS Optimization Feedback — April 25, 2026

## Critical Bug Fixes
1. **3D Velocity Context (Incomplete Navigation)**
   - **Change**: Added `vz_norm` (normalized Z-velocity) to the observation vector. 
   - **Meaning**: Previously, the satellite only "saw" its movement in the X and Y planes. Because the orbit is polar-inclined (97.7°), it was missing roughly 1/3 of its orbital context. Adding Z-velocity allows the AI to correctly orient its prograde/retrograde thrust in 3D ECI space.
   - **Impact**: Dramatically improves thrust efficiency and prevents "drifting" into re-entry.

2. **Solar Cycle Generalization (Training Robustness)**
   - **Change**: Implemented `smas_set_time` in the C++ engine and added `randomize=True` to the Python `reset()` call.
   - **Meaning**: The AI now trains against random dates between 2000 and 2017. 
   - **Impact**: The satellite is no longer "surprised" by sudden high-drag periods (Solar Maximum) that it hadn't seen during static-date training.

## Reward Shaping Updates
1. **Aggressive Altitude Maintenance**
   - `w_alt`: Increased from 10.0 to 100.0. (Stronger correction signal).
   - `alt_deadband_km`: Reduced from 10.0km to 2.0km. (Strict 600km station-keeping).
2. **Survival Prioritization**
   - `w_fuel`: Reduced from 5.0 to 1.0. (Telling the AI that using fuel for survival is "cheaper" than allowing altitude to drop).
   - `w_fatal`: Increased from 1,000 to 5,000. (Extreme discouragement of re-entry).

## Technical Requirements
- **Observation Space**: Increased from 29 to 30 dimensions.
- **Engine Rebuild**: C++ DLL must be re-compiled to support the `smas_set_time` export.
- **Retraining**: Models must be retrained using the new 30-dim observation builder.

## Premium Visuals & Dashboard (Phase 4)
1. **Dynamic Lighting & Glint**
   - **Update**: Added Blinn-Phong specular glint (ocean reflection) and Rayleigh scattering (atmospheric bloom).
   - **Impact**: Sunlight now feels directional and "clear." The Earth looks like a physical object rather than a flat sphere.
2. **Sun Starburst & Cosmos**
   - **Update**: Implemented procedural Sun rays and a static, sparse starfield.
   - **Impact**: Fixes the "TV Static" look of the background and adds cinematic "light rays" as requested.
3. **Procedural Continents**
   - **Update**: Added noise-based landmass generation locked to the Earth's local normal.
   - **Impact**: The Earth now rotates realistically with visible landmasses and the SAA heatmap in sync.

## Mission Agent Optimization (Phase 5)
1. **The "Lazy AI" / Sloth Fix**
   - **Change**: Increased `w_valid_target` (50 -> 500) and added `w_sloth_penalty` (-200).
   - **Meaning**: The AI was choosing to stay in Deep Sleep forever to avoid power loss. We have now made "Imaging" more valuable than "Sleeping."
   - **Impact**: Satellite will now wake up and prioritize the mission over pure survival when the battery is healthy (>90%).
2. **Offline Analysis Tools**
   - **Feature**: Created `visualize.py`.
   - **Benefit**: Allows you to generate high-resolution PNG plots and mission summary CSVs (Lifetime, Fuel, Payload duty cycle) after each run.
